About Me

During my early high school days, when I received my first computer, my brother became captivated by the Spiderman 2 game. However, his progress was short-lived as I inadvertently crashed the operating system before he could even reach level 3.

Initially, I believed that technology wasn't meant for me, but this incident unintentionally unveiled my passion for it. Little did I know that this would pave the way for my career as a software engineer.

Today, I still hold a deep admiration for technology, and if given the chance, I would break that computer all over again. :)
