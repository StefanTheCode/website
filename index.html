About Me

On the first computer I got in my life, in my early high school days, my brother was fascinated by the game of the eponymous hero Spiderman 2. Unfortunately, he didn't even reach level 3, because out of curiosity I crashed the operating system.

At that moment, I thought that this wonderful device was not for me, but I had no idea that this small detail in my life would accidentally reveal a part of myself that is in love with technology. As well as the fact that in my early beginnings, it will define my career path.

And here I am, a software engineer, still in love with technology. When the time came back, I would break the computer again. :)
